Another important reason is entertainment. Many of my friends use TikTok or Instagram when they feel tired or bored. They watch short videos, funny posts, or lifestyle content to relax after school.

 

Besides that, some people also use social media to get information. For instance, they follow news accounts, university pages, or study-related accounts. So overall, I would say social media is useful for communication, entertainment, and learning new things."

 

10

Interesting. Can you share a positive experience you or someone you know has had while using social media?

 

参考答案

 

"Yes, I once had a positive experience using social media. Last year, I joined a study group on WeChat before an important exam. In the group, classmates shared notes, useful websites, and reminders about deadlines.

 

It was very helpful because I could ask questions quickly when I did not understand something. Sometimes my classmates explained difficult points in a simple way, which made me feel less stressed.

 

Because of that group, I prepared better for the exam and felt more confident. So for me, social media can be very useful when people use it for learning and support."

参考答案

 

"In my opinion, social media makes face-to-face connection easier, as long as people use it properly. For example, my friends and I often use WeChat to decide when and where to meet. Without social media, it would be harder to organize group plans quickly.

 

Also, social media helps people stay updated about each other’s lives. If I see that a friend is free or has returned to my city, I may message him and suggest meeting in person.

 

Of course, some people may spend too much time online and avoid real conversations. But overall, I think social media is more helpful than harmful because it makes communication faster and helps people arrange face-to-face meetings more easily."
