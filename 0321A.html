I recently completed a stay at your hotel, and while the service was satisfactory, the room's features were far below expectations. The primary issues involved a lack of hot water in the shower during the mornings and a very unstable Wi-Fi connection, which hindered my ability to stay connected.

 

As a result, my stay was much less comfortable than anticipated. Having to take cold showers in the morning was incredibly unpleasant and started each day on a negative note. Additionally, the poor internet connection meant I could not research local attractions or manage my travel bookings, which caused a great deal of stress.

 

Because the room’s condition did not match the "luxury" experience advertised on your website, I am requesting a formal apology and some form of compensation, such as a credit toward my bill. I look forward to hearing how you plan to address this matter.
