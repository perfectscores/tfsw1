I also try to limit my intake of processed foods and sugary drinks, especially during the week. For lunch and dinner, I eat a lot of chicken breast and eggs because they are excellent sources of protein. I usually pair them with a large portion of steamed vegetables like broccoli or carrots. I also love snacking on nuts and yogurt instead of chips or candy. These foods are a regular part of my life because they are simple to prepare but very nutritious."

Interesting. Tell me about a time when you or someone you know made a significant change to their diet. What was the change and what motivated you or the person you know to make it?

I read that too much sugar can cause energy crashes, so I wanted to see if I could improve my focus. At first, it was very difficult because I had strong cravings, but I replaced sweets with fresh fruit and nuts. After about a month, I noticed a huge difference. I felt much more energized and alert during my classes. It was a tough change, but it really improved my overall health and quality of life."

参考答案
